A Comprehensive Guide to Understanding Asthma

What is Asthma?

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition characterized by inflammation and narrowing of the airways. Recurring episodes of wheezing, breathlessness, chest tightness, and coughing are common symptoms.

Symptoms of Asthma:

  1. Wheezing: A distinctive high-pitched whistling sound, often heard during exhalation.
  2. Shortness of Breath: Difficulty getting enough air into the lungs, leading to breathlessness.
  3. Coughing: Persistent coughing, particularly at night or early morning.
  4. Complications: Untreated asthma can result in serious complications, including heart disease, stroke, kidney damage, and other vascular issues.
  5. Chest Tightness: Described as a sensation of pressure or tightness in the chest.

If you have concerns about your respiratory health, seek consultation with our healthcare professionals. Accurate diagnosis and a personalized treatment plan await you.
